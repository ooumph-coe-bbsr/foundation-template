## How to use this file

1. Create **one entry per significant AI interaction**, using the template below.
2. Give each entry a **Session ID** in the format `PL-<module>-<number>` (e.g. `PL-m01-001`).
3. Reference that ID in the **Prompt Log Ref** column of your `AI_USAGE.md`.
4. Paste your **exact prompt** — this is the evidence of your thinking. For the response, paste the **key part or a short summary**; you don't need the entire reply. Never invent or change a prompt you didn't actually send.
5. Keep entries in date order. When you refine a prompt, log the improved version as the **next entry** (`-001` → `-002`) so the improvement is visible.
